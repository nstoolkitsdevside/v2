What is Metascraper?

Metascraper is a high-efficiency extraction engine designed to instantly capture and format webpage metadata. It can also clean data such as removing .ph or Philippines and lets you copy or download it using a hotkey. | Default hotkey Ctrl+Shift+Z (copy) or Ctrl+Shift+Q to download.
